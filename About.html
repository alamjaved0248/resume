Computer Science Engineering Why did I chose this domain?

I chose computer science as a major because it allows me to pursue both of my interests: problem solving and creating creative experiences. I can generate ideas for solving user-specific problems, design and ideate possible solutions, and then execute them using code. Finally, participating in this end-to-end experience is rewarding for me because I get to not only imagine an experience but also bring it to life.

Coding and Programming Love for coding

Because coding tasks are non-repetitive, you are constantly learning and challenging yourself! You constantly broaden your understanding of the problem and the solution while developing a programme or app, experimenting with new frameworks, solving tricky problems with new algorithms, and employing a different methodology. All of this helps to stretch your mind while also improving your patience, perseverance, and discipline. In summary, coding will undoubtedly take you outside of your comfort zone, but in a good way!
